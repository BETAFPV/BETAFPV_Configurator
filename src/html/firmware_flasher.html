Warning

Please do not try to flash non-Cetus/LiteSilver hardware with this firmware flasher.
Do not disconnect the board or turn off your computer while flashing.

Note: The bootloader is stored in ROM, it cannot be bricked.


IMPORTANT: Ensure you flash a file appropriate for your target. Flashing a binary for the wrong target can cause bad things to happen.

Steps of how to update the firmware

Follow these steps to update the firmware for your flight control:

  • Power off
  • Hold button on the FC
  • Power on(activity LED will NOT flash if done correctly)
  • Connect flight control to computer
  • Load Firmware and then Flash firmware
